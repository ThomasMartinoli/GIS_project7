About us

This project of comparing and validating the two world population maps was done on the region of Siberia as highlighted by the tiles in the map. The main project included the whole world, the rest parts of the world were splitted and distributed on other teams working for the same purpose. The region of our work was defined by Sub-Area 7, it is possible to view the results of our study by going to Sub-Area 7 in the Earth tab in the sidebar. The study was performed by Zhijun Hu, Thomas Martinoli and Sami Miaari.
